Press the Power button and open your palm to turn on the air condition.

Press the Power button and close your palm to turn off the air condition.

Press the Temperature button and do the Thumbs Up gesture to increase the temperature of the air condition.

Press the Temperature button and do the Thumbs Down gesture to decrease the temperature of the air condition.

Press the Mode button and do the I Love You gesture to change the mode of the air condition.

Press the Fan button and do the Victory gesture to change the fan mode of the air condition.
